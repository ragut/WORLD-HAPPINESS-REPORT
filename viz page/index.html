Interactive World Happiness Report
The world happiness report is a survey in almost all the countries of the world, which measures the happiness of people in that country, this score is divided into several indexes that explain it, such as corruption, or life expectancy. The project consists in making a visualization of the data of the world happiness report, understandable and interactive, striking for the users, who are common people who want to explore the report, either for entertainment or for research.

DIARY

What happened in this project.

  • September 13- September 30

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Interest in this project was established, given the particularity of the topic and the multiple possibilities about visualization. It is a topic that we find entertaining to work and interesting, besides that already has a prestige and this could bring different opportunities for us. First contact established with Mario.

  • October 03

    First meeting

    We identified with the hand of Mario his main target: happiness ranking and importance that anyone can understand it. Everyone!

  • October 05-October 30

    Thinking and designing

    We started to think what could be the best option for our problem. We constructed the first alternative and with feedback of our profesors,we met a good proposal with a choroplet map, parallel coordinates and bars!

  • October 31

    Another meeting with our client

    Our advance was show it! Mario saw the first approach and told us his opinion about that, defining a little details.

  • November 1-17

    More feedback

    A second feedback was given by our professors, these days we explore a little details to include and improve the current design. !

  • November 18

    Talking with Claire

    Claire is an expert in the Report, so it was necessary her opinion to confirm we were on the right way, fortunately we were!

  • The happiest
    visualization
    is born!
